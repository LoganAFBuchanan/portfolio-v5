Monster Sad!

Designer/Programmer/Artist

Monster Sad! is a game about a depressed troll I designed and coded in Lua using the PICO-8 game engine for the 33rd Ludum Dare game jam competition in August of 2015.

Slaw & Slawter

Lead Programmer/Designer
