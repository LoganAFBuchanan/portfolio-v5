Ethos

My portfolio aims to present an ethos that is clean, professional, and diverse. In order to stand out from the typical game developer audience I avoid visual cliches like pixelated type, gaming iconography, and garish design. Instead I focus on straight to the point copy, that is supported by clean easily navigable projects. “I make games” is the clear mantra I put forth.
