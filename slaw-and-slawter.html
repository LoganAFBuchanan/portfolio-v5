Slaw & Slawter

Lead Programmer / Designer

Tools: C#, Unity

Timeline: 4 Months

Team: Rupneet Atwal, Jarielle Lim, John Limiac

Overview

Slaw and Slawter is a game that follows the life of Amos, a retired adventurer who has opened a tavern in a small village. One night, he has a dream that a dark energy will take over the town and the townspeople. To combat the forces, Amos must collect runestones from the Western and Eastern swamps in order to unlock the Northern Caves and stop the unknown forces of evil.

Ideation

The initial idea of the game sprouted from a discussion on limited inventory space amongst the team. We wanted to explore a game world where what you decide to take with you had real consequences in the moment to moment gameplay, as well as with larger progression systems. This lead us to the idea of a tired and worn out adventurer who no longer has the strength they once had.

Issues

The primary issue we ran into with Slaw and Slawter was that our scope was too large for the project. In addition to a fast combat system we also had, an economy of enemy drops used for income, an extensive shop full of upgrades, 3 enemy types spread across 2 zones culminating in a final boss encounter, and much more.

Polish and Completion

In the end, we had very little time for polish and our game, while fun, was a bit rough around the edges.
